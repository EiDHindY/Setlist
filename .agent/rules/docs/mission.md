# 🚀 App Mission & Vision: Setlist

> [!NOTE]
> Setlist is more than just an app; it's a living hub for music lovers to compete, discover, and celebrate the songs they love.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Music is often a shared experience, but there's no dedicated, interactive space to "play" with your music collection. Our founder, DoD, started this as a game with his wife (Lola) to pick songs and make them compete, but the spark ignited a vision for a global musical hub.

## 🦸 The Solution

Setlist acts as the ultimate hub for music-related features. It starts with the "Song Competition" game—a place to pick, battle, and save songs—and aims to grow into a massive ecosystem for everything revolving around the music we love.

## ✨ Tone & Vibe

* **Solarized Dark:** A sophisticated, high-contrast but easy-on-the-eyes palette. 
* **Alive & Soulful:** Highly interactive with fluid animations (Framer Motion). The app should feel like it's breathing and responding to every touch.
* **Metal Spirit:** Built for those who love the energy and passion of music, especially the metal community.

## 👥 Target Audience

The millions of music enthusiasts around the world who don't just "listen" to music, but live it.
